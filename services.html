For Employers

Talent Sourcing & Screening

We maintain an extensive database of skilled professionals from Pakistan, from which our team screens and provides the best candidates for employer screening.

Candidate Vetting & Interviews

We handle the initial vetting and interview process, saving you time and effort. Our team ensures that only the most qualified candidates are presented, based on your specifications.

Visa & Documentation Support

Navigating international hiring regulations can be complex. We offer visa assistance, documentation support, and compliance with the legal requirements of both Pakistan and Gulf countries to make the transition smooth for both the candidate and employer.

Post-Hire Support

Our commitment to successful placements doesn't stop at hiring. We provide support to both employers and candidates after the job offer, ensuring the transition is successful and that both parties are satisfied.

CV Enhancement & Interview Preparation

We assist in optimizing your resume to meet the standards of Gulf employers and provide interview preparation to ensure you present yourself confidently and professionally.

Visa & Relocation Assitance

Moving abroad can be overwhelming, but we're here to help. We assist candidates with the visa process, work permits, and other documentation, ensuring a smooth relocation to the Gulf.
